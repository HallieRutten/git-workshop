What are IMDb ratings? IMDb registered users can cast a vote (from 1 to 10) on every released title in the database. Individual votes are then aggregated and summarized as a single IMDb rating, visible on the titles main page.

Metascore is considered the rating of a film. Scores are assigned to movies reviews of large group of the worlds most respected critics, and weighted average are applied to summarize their opinions range. The result is shown in single number that captures the essence of critical opinion in one Metascore.

movies <- movies %>% 
  mutate( hours = round(runtime/60,1),
          mill = gross/1000000,
          mil10 = round(mill/10),
          mil100 = round(mill/100) )
